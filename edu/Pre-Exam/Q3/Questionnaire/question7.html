Question 7

Your network contains an Active Directory Domain Services (AD DS) domain named contoso.com. The domain contains two sites named Site1 and Site2. You perform the following actions:

  • At 09:00 AM, in Site1, User1 adds User2 to Group1.
  • At 09:30 AM, in Site2, an administrator removes User2 from Group1.
  • At 10:00 AM, in Site1, User3's phone number is set to 333-333.
  • At 10:30 AM, in Site2, User3 is deleted.

For each of the following statements, select Yes if the statement is true. Otherwise, select No.

At 11:30 AM, User1 & User2 are members of Group1:


At 11:30 AM, the phone number of User2 is 333-333:


At 11:30 AM, User3 is deleted:


Explanation:

1. No - The update in Site1 at 09:00 AM was overwritten by the update in Site2 at 09:30 AM.
2. No - The phone number update was for User3, not User2.
3. Yes - User3 was deleted in Site2 at 10:30 AM.

Correct Answer:

1. No
2. No
3. Yes
