Question 5

Your company uses Storage Spaces Direct.

You need to view the available storage in a Storage Space Direct storage pool.

What should you use?





Explanation: Open the Windows Admin Center web interface in your browser.
Connect to the server running the Storage Spaces Direct cluster.
In the left navigation pane, click on "Storage Spaces Direct" under the "Storage" section.
Click on the "Pools" tab to see a list of the storage pools in the cluster.
Select the storage pool you want to view and click on the "Usage" tab.
The "Usage" tab will show you a breakdown of the available storage in the pool, including the total capacity, the amount of storage in use, and the amount of free space.

Your Answer:

Correct Answer: D. Windows Admin Center
